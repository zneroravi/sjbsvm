Welcome

Founded in 1985, Shriji Baba Saraswati Vidya Mandir is located in Mathura, Uttar Pradesh, India. Considered among the top rung of Indian Public Schools, Shriji Baba Saraswati Vidya Mandir, Mathura popularly known as SJBSVM Mathura, is a Boarding-cum-Day school for Boys and Girls. Over the last three decades, SJBSVM has grown to a student strength of 2000 and staff strength of 120. The school prepares students for the AISSE (Class X) and the AISSCE (Class XII) Examinations conducted by the Central Board of Secondary Education, New Delhi.
